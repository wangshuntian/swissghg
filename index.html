📖 Key Terms

Production Emissions
GHG emitted by all Swiss industries (territorial), regardless of whether the output is consumed domestically or exported.
Supply-Chain Emissions
GHG emitted worldwide across all upstream supply chains to produce goods and services consumed by Swiss final demand (via MRIO).
Direct Consumption
GHG directly emitted by Swiss final consumers: households, NPISH, and government.
Total Consumption Footprint
Supply-chain + direct consumption emissions = total GHG attributable to Swiss final demand.
Footprint Multiplier
Total consumption footprint ÷ production emissions. Ratio > 1 means Switzerland is a net importer of embodied emissions.
Import Dependency
Share of a sector's consumption footprint that originates from foreign production. Higher ratio = more reliant on imported emissions.
Production Export Share
Fraction of Swiss production emissions embodied in goods/services consumed by other countries.
